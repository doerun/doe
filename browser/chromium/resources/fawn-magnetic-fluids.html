Stylized Magnetic Demo

Magnetic Fluids

Not sold as strict ferrofluid physics. This is a compute-updated height field driven by moving magnetic wells and rendered with prismatic, high-dispersion shading so the surface feels metallic, unstable, and alive. Move or hold the pointer to drag a stronger magnet through it.
